# Working style

I work **remote-first and async-friendly**. No relocation is needed, and I'm happy to travel occasionally for kickoffs or on-site weeks.

I take **cross-stack ownership** — web, backend, data, mobile, and cloud/infra — rather than handing off at the API boundary. At my current company I've personally owned everything from the AI customer-service platform to the mobile app releases and the GCP → Alibaba Cloud migration.

I also **ramp up on new technology fast**. At my first job I was given two weeks to learn the stack (OutSystems) and was productive in three days — I pick up new tools and domains quickly, which is how I've moved across web, payments, infra, and AI.
